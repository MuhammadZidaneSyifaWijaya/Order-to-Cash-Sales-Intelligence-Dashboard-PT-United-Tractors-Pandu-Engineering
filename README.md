# 🚜 Order-to-Cash & Sales Intelligence Dashboard — PT United Tractors Pandu Engineering

## 📌 Project Overview
Delivered a 4-page Sales & Marketing Dashboard in Power BI to monitor and optimize the Order-to-Cash (O2C) cycle. The solution covers key operational domains including Ship Building, Repair, Product Support, and Marketing Overview, serving as the central analytical tool for the Marketing team to track pipeline targets and sales activities in real-time.

## 🗺️ Business Process Architecture (BPMN)
Mapped the complete Order-to-Cash (O2C) process as a **10-step, 16-actor Business Process Model Notation (BPMN) diagram**. This represents the first structured documentation of this workflow and was formally adopted by the team as its digitalization reference.

## 🏗️ Data Architecture, Pipeline & Impact

1. **⚙️ Data Cleansing & ETL Pipeline:** 
   Built a robust ETL pipeline in Excel and Power Query processing **55,000+ sales quotations and CRM records**. Successfully resolved **1,500+ NULL/ERROR values**.
2. **🗃️ Data Modeling:** 
   Restructured relational table schemas to produce the clean, validated, and analysis-ready dataset that powered the final visualization layer.
3. **📊 Visualization & Analytics:** 
   Developed the 4-page Power BI dashboard detailing metrics across Ship Building, Repair, Product Support, and overall Marketing.
4. **🎯 Business Impact:** 
   Successfully **eliminated a 2-week monthly manual reporting cycle**, providing 4 Marketing team members with direct, real-time visibility into pipeline targets and sales operations.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack Used
* **Business Intelligence:** Power BI (DAX, Power Query)
* **Process Modeling:** BPMN (Business Process Model and Notation)
* **Data Processing:** Microsoft Excel
* **Domain Expertise:** Sales Analytics, Order-to-Cash (O2C) Cycle, CRM Integration
